OVERVIEW

At Cloud ARS, We measure our trial customers on the basis of their behavioral patterns such as their "Age" , "Average Daily Time Spent" , "Number of Virtual Machines Deployed", "Top Hit URL's Categories" during their trial phase. Firstly, we massaged the data as we had a large dataset from previous project (Data Gurus.com), we did clean the data with pandas for ML perspective, then we performed Exploratory Data Analysis(EDA) to gauge some most important features for model training purposes. Finally, We created a Machine Learning Model by Logistic Regression to predict the Potential Customers out of the trial customers. Note: The dataset we used for training already included some Potential Customers, previously were Trial Customers. Please navigate on the NavBar to test the accuracy of MODEL ARS.
